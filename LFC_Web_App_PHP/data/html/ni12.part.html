Liverpool enter race for Real Madrid midfielder

Liverpool have reportedly entered the race to sign Real Madrid starlet Dani Ceballos.

Ceballos has caught the eye at the U21s European Championships this summer but he has no future under Zinedine Zidane at the Bernabeu.

Arsenal and Tottenham are both believed to be keeping tabs on the 22-year-old, with AC Milan also in the mix and Real not ruling out a loan move.

But reports in Spain suggest Liverpool are also keen on Ceballos.

Marca claims Liverpool have been keeping an eye on the midfielder with their interest growing in recent weeks.

While Jürgen Klopp was not expected to do much business this summer, it is suggested he believes Ceballos would add an extra dimension to the Liverpool midfield.
