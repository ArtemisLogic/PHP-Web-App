Monster energy drink addiction ravages nation in wake of Liverpool Champions League win.

As Liverpool won the Champions League, the squad were all pictured to have been drinking Monster energy drink in celebration.

As a result sales of the soft drink have spiked all over the North West and people have even been reported to camp outside supermarkets as the supply dwindles.

One man told our reporter he had been awake for 69 hours as he huddled outside of Tesco at 5am waiting to get his Monster fix.

Monster's Inc have declined to respond to the issue.
